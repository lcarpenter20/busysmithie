    The Roost, one of the farthest cafes from campus, has a great atmosphere if you want to sit down and have some coffee. However, after talking to some of our peers, this cafe is 1) too loud, 2) too busy, and 3) wifi deficient. Every time we've gone to this cafe, regardless of the weekend or weekday, there have been quite a lot of people during the day. They also play loud music, so if you're able to drown out noise easily, this cafe could work; otherwise, you're not in luck. There are multiple tables with different table sizes, so it is very accommodating for group or solo work!
