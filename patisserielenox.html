    Lenox Patisserie, with its chic interior design, is one of our favorite places to study: it got four stars straight across the board! The lighting, great for studying, isn't too bright or too dim. Again, there is a variety of table sizes for either individual work or group work. One drawback of this cafe, however, is its wifi. It took a while to connect to the wifi, and even when trying to connect to Smith's server via VPN, we could not successfully connect. So, if you're doing some simple readings, review of notes, or work that doesn't necessarily require wifi, we would highly recommend this place to study!
