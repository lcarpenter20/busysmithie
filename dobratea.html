    Dobra Tea was one of the most "vibiest" cafes we visited. With its moody lights, interior decoration, and teas, what's not to love about Dobra Tea? This place is great for deep conversations with your best friends, but maybe not so much for studying. The lighting is a bit dark so if you need to do some readings.. it may not be the best place to study. There are a variety of tables, large and small, so if you just need to hash out some group work plans over tea, this is your place! There's a lot of space in Dobra Tea, so it is highly unlikely that you won't be able to find a place to sit.
