    Tart Baking Company, although very pretty on the inside and perfect for your instagram's aesthetic, doesn't serve much as a studying place. There are only two seating areas, or more like standing areas. Even though it is possible to study here, it would be really difficult to study in a group setting. When we visited, there were only two chairs, so if you need to do some group work... this is not your study space. That being said, you could work alone while looking out onto Main Street. The bright lights are perfect for studying too!
