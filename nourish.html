    Nourish, right across The Roost, has a great selection of food. However, as a place to study, it may not be the best. There are limited table spaces so regardless of group studying or independent studying, we would recommend getting there early to secure a seat. There is a lot of natural light that comes into the cafe, so lighting isn't an issue. The cafe itself has a very quiet vibe, and doesn't play loud music. It is an ideal place to study besides its lack of table space and distance from campus! We would recommend getting their acai bowl if you come here.
