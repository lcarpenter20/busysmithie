    Hey market! You’ll miss Haymarket if you don’t keep an eye out for it! Haymarket, with its two floors, allows for many customers to come in and study or socialize. There isn’t much room on the first floor, but going downstairs you have everything! There are many tables, large and small, so it’s perfect for if you want to work alone or in a group setting. However, the lighting isn't the greatest. If you really want a brightly lit study space, this probably isn't the place for you. That being said, the downstairs area has more light than the first floor, but still isn't the best place if you want bright, white lights. Also if you are vegetarian, this is the perfect place for you! We recommend getting one of their delicious smoothies!
