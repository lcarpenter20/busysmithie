    Northampton Coffee is one of the farthest cafes from Smith's campus! You have to walk down a street off of Main, for a bit, to reach this coffee shop. When first walking in, the main area has some small tables and one large table; but, walking into the side room allows for brighter lights, more table spaces, and a quiet atmosphere! Even though there was some soft music playing in the background, it was nothing to distract someone from studying. The vibe there was very serene and quiet; not many people were talking and many were studying/doing work. Overall, this place is a great place to study -- the only downside is its location!
