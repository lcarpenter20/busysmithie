    Woodstar, one of the closest cafes to Smith’s campus, is one of the most frequented cafes in Noho - and with good reason too! We found that Woodstar provided everything needed to be a solid study place. The lighting at Woodstar differed from area to area, but was bright enough to do readings. There were many people who were just sitting and drinking some coffee, but there were also students we recognized doing their school work. Also, the table space: big and spacious! Especially the tables near where you order -- they were large and spacious, so perfect for group work. Also in the summer, it’d be great to chill outside on the seats provided but given that Northampton is cold nine out of the 12 months… we recommend drinking a hot drink inside. Woodstar gets pretty busy during the weekends so we would recommend going early or going during the weekday!
